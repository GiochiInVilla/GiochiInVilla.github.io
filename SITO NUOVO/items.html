Items

Items are multipurpose items that you can find and use.

Equipments Packs

The starting equipment you get from your class includes a collection of useful adventuring gear, put together in a pack. The contents of these packs are listed here. If you are buying your starting equipment, you can purchase a pack for the price shown, which might be cheaper than buying the items individually.

Burglar's Pack

Contain 1000 ball bearings, 10ft of string, a bell, 5 candles, a crowbar, a hammer, 10 pitons, a hooded lantern, 2 flasks of oil, 5 days rations, a tinderbox, a waterskin and 50ft of rope.

Diplomat's Pack

 Includes a chest, 2 cases for maps and scrolls, a set of fine clothes, a bottle of ink, an ink pen, a lamp, 2 flasks of oil, 5 sheets of paper, a vial of perfume, sealing wax, and soap.

Dungeoneer's Pack

 Includes a backpack, a crowbar, a hammer, 10 pitons, 10 torches, a tinderbox, 10 days of rations, a waterskin and 50ft of rope.

Entertainer's Pack

 Includes a backpack, a bedroll, 2 costumes, 5 candles, 5 days of rations, a waterskin, and a disguise kit.

Explorer's Pack

 Includes a backpack, a bedroll, a mess kit, a tinderbox, 10 torches, 10 days of rations, a waterskin and 50ft of rope.

Priest's Pack

 Includes a backpack, a blanket, 10 candles, a tinderbox, an alms box, 2 blocks of incense, a censer, vestments, 2 days of rations, and a waterskin.

Scholar's Pack

 Includes a backpack, a book of lore, a bottle of ink, an ink pen, 10 sheets of parchment, a little bag of sand, and a small knife.

Metals and Minerals

The finest metals and minerals give some excellent skills for the adventurers' equipment.

Darkwood

 500 Eres / lb. This rare magic wood is as hard as normal wood but very light. Any wooden item made from darkwood is considered a masterwork item and weighs only half as much as normal. A shield in darkwood don't cause disadvantage in stealth checks.

Copper

 25 Eres / lb.

Bronze

 50 Eres / lb. Light and one-handed weapons can be crafted from bronze. Bronze is too weak to be used for two-handed weapons. Bronze weapons do the same damage as steel weapons of the same type, and have the same cost and weight but are more fragile.
Bronze can be used to create any medium or light armor. It protects a creature as well as steel armor does, has the same cost and weight, but it is more fragile.

Dragonhide

 Armor x2 One dragon produces enough hide for a single suit of masterwork hide armor for a medium size creature. Because dragonhide armor isn’t made of metal, druids can wear it without penalty. A suit of dragonhide armor grant resistance to damage in base of the dragon type.

 15K Eres / lb. Suspendium is a mineral capable of floating using magic. The higher the purity of Suspendium, the better it floats. Skyship are able to navigate in the air thank to this material. However it is produced only by the Dwarfs.

Adamantine

 Ammunition +3000 Eres
Light armor +50K Eres
Medium armor +120K Eres
Heavy armor +200K Eres
Weapon +30K Eres		 Adamantine is extremely strong and favored by weapon and armor smiths alike for its ability to cut through solid barriers with ease and endure heavy blows. Armor made from adamantine grants its wearer damage reduction of 1 if it’s light armor, 2 if it’s medium armor, and 3 if it’s heavy armor. Thus, adamantine weapons and ammunition have a +2 enhancement bonus on attack rolls, and the AC of adamantine armor is increased by 1.

Gemstones

Gemstones can be absorbed, however they cannot be removed after being absorbed. The bonus only becomes effective afterwards absorption. To absorb them it takes a ritual during the eclipse and a Wisdom Saving with DC 10. If failed after absorption the player starts going for a short time period in a murderous fury devastating everything You can absorb one gemstone from level 1. At level 7 you gain the capacity to absorb another one. At level 15 you can absorb a third one. By absorbing a gemstone your ability score can exceed 20, but no more than 22.
