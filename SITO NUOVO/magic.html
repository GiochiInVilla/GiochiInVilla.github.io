Downtime

Gospels

Ancient artifacts that hide a secret and powerful curse. Although associated with a disadvantage, they allow for the authority of sin.

Gospels Info

Envy - Faye

Your companions will attempt to take this item from you.

Greed - Nairom

 The user start to be more possessive and doesn't want to share what he got.

Wrath - Satella

When you deal a killing blow, you must immediately attack a random creature close to you.

Sloth - Shaosi

 Your move speed is decreased by 10ft.

Gluttony - Saoko

 The user is always hungry. If he didn't eat for 12h he become feral and aggressive, stopping at nothing to get food.

Pride - Azenu

 You must use your fist turn in combat moving as close as you can toward the closest enemy. You cannot retreat form combat.

Lust - Cadner

 You gain disadvantage to Charisma rolls.
