The Deck of Tarot

Mystical cards endowed with the most unimaginable powers. They have a big secret and a big curse above them. Each card is scattered all over the world in search of a master and once found it cannot separate from it except with death.

The Fool

You can no more orientate. You can't find the north, or can't remember any path you walk on. You have disadvantage in survival check.

The Magician

You lean a cantrip of your choice from any spell pool.

The High Priestess

You gain +2 ATK bonus against aberration, fey, fiend, or undead creatures. You have advantage against their saving throws.

The Empress

You stats to see everyone around you as an enemy, you start becoming paranoid and can't trust nobody. You gain 1d4 Insanity for each day you keep this card.

The Emperor

You gain proficiency to intimidation checks. If you already have it, it is doubled. You have resistance to being intimidated.

The Hierophant

You gain proficiency to wisdom. If you already have it is doubled while you keep this card.

The Lovers

Your shadow curse you. While your shadow is visible, so when there is light, your saving throws have a -2 penalty.

The Chariot

Once a day you can use Misty Step without consuming a spell slot.

Strength

You gain a +5 bonus in Initiative. You are immune to be frightened.

The Hermit

You lose all the money you have and you keep losing them, while you have this card.

Wheel of Fortune

Each 1 you roll in attack you roll a d100 from the wild magic table.

Justice

You will be sent to another plane of existence onwards the guardian and he will judge you based on your action and he can decide to either give you a contract with some power or cursing you.

The Hanged Man

You became wanted in every city. There is a bounty on your head.

Death

You can cheat your death by dueling against it.

Temperance

You have a 20% bonus EXP gain.

The Devil

Your alignment is shifted to evil.

The Tower

You gain a compass giving you the secret entrance to the tower.

The Star

You can orientate everywhere. You have Advantage in survival check.

The Moon

During a full moon you become a lycanthrope. During this time you became chaotic evil, killing anything.

The Sun

The gods give you 1 inspiration to use each eclipse.

Judgement

In the current plane your nemesis start chaise you. It's level and strength scale with you.

The World

You are granted True Vision, while holding this card.
